Accepted file formats & how hubs are matched

Per file: FMS zoning geojson (customized_zone_list), a standard GeoJSON FeatureCollection, or a JSON list of records with zone_geo_polygon WKT. Zones are related by geographic overlap only — zone names are never used for the comparison (they appear only as labels). Hubs are paired by station name; hubs whose names don't match are paired by location instead.
